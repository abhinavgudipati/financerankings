Finance Rankings

A metrics-based ranking of global finance research universities based on faculty publications in leading journals, weighted by authorship credit and filtered by year and journal selection.

👉 Click the ▶ icon to expand institutions and faculty.

Rankings are computed using faculty publications in the following journals: Journal of Finance (JF), Journal of Financial Economics (JFE), and Review of Financial Studies (RFS).

Each paper contributes fractional authorship credit based on the number of coauthors. Institutional scores are the sum of faculty credit within the selected time window.

Citation counts are displayed separately and do not directly affect ranking unless “Sort by Citations” is selected.

Filters allow users to adjust the year range, journal selection, country, and sorting method dynamically.
